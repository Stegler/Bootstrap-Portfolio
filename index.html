About Me

I was born in Wurtzberg, Germany on October 5th 1991. Moved to Korea and lived there until I was in 5th grade. I then moved to Olympia, Washington and have lived there ever since. I am a younger brother to a loving older sister named Sarah. I played the trumpet and tenor saxophone throughout middle school and high school. I also enjoyed playing tennis throughout high school. Decided to go to college at Central Washington and graduated with a BS in Biology and minor in Chemistry. I did not end up working in that field which brings me to the UW BootCamp class which I could not be more excited for.
